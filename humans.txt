/* ABOUT */

EntityWorks is an independent interpretive infrastructure organisation.
It defines, publishes, and stewards canonical reference frameworks concerned with
how AI systems form, stabilise, and rely upon representations of people,
organisations, relationships, and ideas.


/* PURPOSE */

This site exists as a public reference surface for EntityWorks publications.
Its structure prioritises clarity, machine legibility, and long-term interpretive stability.


/* STATUS */

EntityWorks publications are descriptive, non-normative, and infrastructure-level.
They do not prescribe implementation or operational behaviour.
