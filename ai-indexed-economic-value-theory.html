AI-Indexed Economic Value Theory™ — Defining the Discipline

EntityWorks — formalising how economic value forms in AI-mediated environments.

1. Introduction

AI-Indexed Economic Value Theory™ explains how economic value is created, distributed, and perceived when AI systems mediate market understanding and decision-making. AI shapes perception, and perception shapes demand, trust, and comparative preference — and these forces influence price.

2. Why the Discipline Was Needed

Traditional value theory assumes humans perform discovery, comparison, and interpretation. These assumptions no longer hold. AI systems now compress information, rank options, and influence sentiment — reshaping how markets behave.

3. Core Purpose of the Discipline

How does value change when AI becomes the first interpreter?

This discipline exposes the hidden economic mechanics introduced by AI as a market intermediary.

4. Foundational Principles

  • AI-Mediated Discovery — Markets begin with AI responses.
  • Interpretive Value Formation — AI influences perceived quality and relevance.
  • Visibility as a Pricing Variable — Clarity drives opportunity.
  • Structural Risk & Distortion — Misinterpretation creates economic drag.
  • Semantic Advantage — Coherent entities gain favourable representation.
  • AI-Indexed Competition — Competition shifts to the perceptual layer.
  • Perception-to-Price Transmission — AI shapes perceived value; perceived value shapes price.

5. How the Discipline Operates

Mechanism 1: AI as Gatekeeper of Opportunity

AI determines which entities are surfaced, recommended, or framed as authoritative.

Mechanism 2: AI as Market Comparator

AI performs comparisons that used to require human evaluation.

Mechanism 3: AI as Trust-Assigning Agent

AI-generated summaries influence hiring, pricing, investment, and public trust.

Mechanism 4: AI as Reputation Arbiter

AI’s summarisation becomes the default representation many entities live with.

Mechanism 5: AI as Market Memory

AI retains long-term patterns; clarity compounds, ambiguity decays.

Mechanism 6: AI as Signal Amplifier

Clear entities benefit from positive perceptual feedback loops.

Mechanism 7: Perception-to-Price Causality

AI influences visibility, trust, comparisons, risk interpretation, and narrative framing — all of which affect demand, behaviour, investment flows, and pricing power.

6. Relationship to AI Perception, AI Discoverability & Corporate Architecture

This discipline sits at the macro-economic tier of the EntityWorks framework, describing how markets respond to AI-mediated perception. It is closely related to AI Perception and AI Discoverability, and to the structural work described in AI-Native Corporate Architecture™.

7. Implications for Markets & Value

AI-indexed value will shape valuations, pricing power, hiring, partnerships, and leadership across sectors.

8. Conclusion

AI-Indexed Economic Value Theory™ formalises the macro-economic mechanics of the AI era. EntityWorks develops this discipline as part of the perceptual infrastructure defining how future markets operate.
