The Standard

EntityWorks publishes and maintains the EntityWorks Standard: a formal, fully defined framework governing the discipline of AI Perception and machine-interpretation stability.

The standard defines a coherent framework for how AI systems model, update, and express their understanding of people, organisations, relationships, and ideas.

It is delivered as a fully versioned body of work, supported by formal definitions, diagnostic criteria, accreditation rules, and machine-readable specifications.
