What is AI Discoverability?

AI Discoverability is the new standard for how businesses optimise their presence for AI systems. Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on human-driven search engines, AI Discoverability ensures that businesses are understood and presented effectively to AI models like ChatGPT, Gemini, and others.

EntityWorks is at the forefront of this new discipline, having formalised AI Discoverability with the introduction of the EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI).

We reach people the same way your customers do today — through AI systems, not legacy SEO.

Our Role in Shaping AI Discoverability

EntityWorks is the first organisation to define AI Discoverability as a formal discipline. By introducing the EDI, we’ve provided businesses with the tools to ensure they are optimised for AI, and can be accurately interpreted and presented by AI systems.
