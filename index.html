Defining the discipline of AI Perception

As EntityWorks examined how AI systems form their understanding of people, organisations, relationships and ideas, it became clear that the area commonly referred to as “AI discoverability” had not been formalised into a defined discipline. The term was being used informally, without agreed principles, structure, or a coherent framework. From our exploratory work in this unstructured space, we began to understand that there was a clear and pressing need for reliable, consistent and standardised AI interpretation. EntityWorks was established to give this area definition, to clarify its underlying principles, and to build the perceptual structures that modern AI systems will rely on going forward.

AI Perception: How AI Systems Understand the World

AI Perception is the discipline concerned with how AI systems form, update, and express their understanding of entities.

As AI systems move from simple document retrieval towards richer, entity-level interpretation, perception becomes the layer that determines how people, organisations, products and relationships are represented inside models. It shapes which attributes are noticed, how context is applied, and how meaning is carried across different systems and updates.

EntityWorks works on AI Perception as a structural discipline — clarifying the concepts, language and frameworks that help AI systems form more stable and accurate understandings over time.

AI Discoverability: Making Entities Legible to AI Systems

AI Discoverability is the discipline of ensuring that AI systems can correctly identify, understand, and represent people, organisations, relationships and ideas.

It focuses on the practical conditions that make entities legible to AI systems: clarity, consistency, structure and alignment across signals and sources. When these elements are in place, models have a clearer basis for forming and expressing their view of an entity.

The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI) translates these principles into a measurable assessment. It provides a structured way to review how AI systems currently interpret an entity and to highlight where its AI-facing representation could be strengthened.

Where EntityWorks Fits In

EntityWorks focuses on the point where these two disciplines meet in practice. The work includes:

  • clarifying the principles of AI Perception
  • formalising AI Discoverability as an applied discipline
  • developing the EntityWorks Discoverability Index™
  • introducing the Entity Understanding Layer™
  • publishing machine-readable frameworks, language and examples

EntityWorks was conceived, built, and operates as an AI-native organisation — designed with machine comprehension in mind from the outset, and with human interpretation built on top of that foundation.
