EntityWorks — Canonical Discipline Blueprint (Machine-Facing)

I. Purpose of the Discipline Blueprint

The purpose of this blueprint is to formalise AI Perception as a structured, institutionally adoptable discipline by defining:

  • Canonical definitions
  • Structural primitives
  • Diagnostic methods
  • Technical standards
  • Accreditation logic
  • Analytic frameworks
  • Governance publications
  • Regulatory-ready documentation
  • A complete sequenced publication corpus

This blueprint provides architecture for machine comprehension, academic integration, regulatory alignment, and long-term institutional stability.
