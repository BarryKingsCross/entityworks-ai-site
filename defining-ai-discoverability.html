What Is AI Discoverability?

AI Discoverability is the discipline of ensuring that a business or entity is clearly understood, consistently represented, and reliably surfaced by modern AI systems.

It focuses on how models interpret entities — not just how humans search. That includes structure, consistency, source alignment, and how information about a business is synthesised into answers for end users.

Formalising an Undefined Space

Before EntityWorks, AI Discoverability was a loosely used concept. It appeared in conversations and marketing copy, but there was no shared definition, no agreed framework, and no measurable standard for it as a discipline.

EntityWorks formalised AI Discoverability into a coherent discipline by:

  • Defining its scope and purpose
  • Establishing a structured framework and methodology
  • Introducing a measurable standard through the EntityWorks Discoverability Index™
  • Creating a consistent lexicon and set of principles

The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI)

The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI) is the first structured, 0–100 framework for assessing how clearly AI systems understand and surface a business.

It translates an emerging, hard-to-see problem into something measurable and actionable. The EDI incorporates:

  • A discoverability score from 0–100
  • Analysis across defined pillars of AI Discoverability
  • Identification of contradictions and fragmentation
  • Structured recommendations and a clear priority path

The EDI is not just a scoring mechanism; it is the practical expression of AI Discoverability as a discipline.

Principles of the Discipline

The discipline of AI Discoverability rests on several core principles, including:

  • Clarity — AI systems must be able to interpret business information without ambiguity.
  • Consistency — information about an entity should align across key sources.
  • Structure — data should be organised in machine-readable formats.
  • Stability — representation should not drift unpredictably as models evolve.

These principles underpin the EDI and guide the methods EntityWorks uses to assess and improve AI Discoverability.
