What is AI Discoverability?

AI Discoverability is the discipline concerned with how AI systems understand, represent, and surface business entities when people ask questions, search, or seek recommendations through AI interfaces.

It goes beyond traditional SEO, which is centred on human-driven search engines and page rankings. AI Discoverability focuses on whether AI systems can form an accurate, coherent internal representation of a business and reliably surface it in the right contexts.

Why AI Discoverability needed to be defined

As AI systems increasingly act as the “front door” to the internet, organisations face new questions:

  • What picture of our organisation do these systems hold?
  • Are we being surfaced when we should be?
  • Are we being confused with competitors or outdated information?

Until now, there has been no formal discipline that names, structures, and measures this space. EntityWorks defines AI Discoverability to give leaders a vocabulary and framework for dealing with it directly.

The role of the EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI)

The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ is the first structured 0–100 framework dedicated to AI Discoverability. It provides an assessment of how clearly and consistently AI systems can understand and surface a business entity.

The EDI is not an SEO metric or a marketing vanity score. It is a machine-comprehension standard for the AI era, rooted in the structural signals that matter most for AI systems.

The role of the Entity Understanding Layer™

The Entity Understanding Layer™ (EUL) is EntityWorks’ interpretive model for how AI systems appear to build and update their internal understanding of entities.

Where the EUL describes the patterns of identification, attribution, contextualisation, weighting, updating, and propagation inside AI systems, the EDI measures how well a given organisation supports those behaviours through its real-world presence.

Discipline-level structure

By defining AI Discoverability as a discipline, EntityWorks brings together:

  • A formal definition and scope
  • The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ as a measurable standard
  • The Entity Understanding Layer™ as an interpretive conceptual model
  • An evolving Lexicon of terms and concepts
  • A methodology for practical audits and implementation

This combination turns a vague concern (“How does AI see us?”) into a structured domain leaders can act on.
