AI Perception — Definition of Record

(As defined within the EntityWorks Standard)

Name: AI Perception

Series: D-Series — Conceptual Definitions

Identifier: D-1000

Status: Published — Definition of Record

Version: v1.0

Publication Date: December 2025

Defining Authority: EntityWorks Ltd, as part of the EntityWorks Standard

Definition (Canonical — Scoped Definition of Record)

Within the EntityWorks Standard, AI Perception is the discipline concerned with how AI systems form, maintain, stabilise, and express representations of people, organisations, relationships, and ideas.

It describes representational behaviour at the level of structure and interpretation, without reference to model internals, implementation techniques, or system design choices.

This definition is authoritative within the scope of the EntityWorks Standard. It does not assert exclusivity or supersede other uses of the term outside that framework.

Purpose

To provide a coherent conceptual framework for describing machine-formed understanding and representational behaviour within AI-mediated environments.

Scope

AI Perception applies to the formation, persistence, transformation, and expression of representations as defined within the EntityWorks Standard.

It does not prescribe system behaviour, technical implementation, or cognitive equivalence.

Conceptual Domain

AI Perception functions as the parent discipline within the EntityWorks Standard. It provides the interpretive foundation upon which downstream constructs — including evaluative indices, diagnostic failure modes, analytical layers, and signalling mechanisms — are situated.

Role Within the EntityWorks Standard

Within the EntityWorks Standard, AI Perception establishes the interpretive domain addressed by the Standard.

It defines the kind of phenomena the Standard concerns itself with, without introducing mechanisms, controls, or prescriptions.

Non-Canonical Uses (Explicit Exclusions)

Within the EntityWorks Standard, AI Perception is not:

  • a claim about AI cognition or consciousness
  • a model of internal system architecture
  • a system design methodology
  • a behavioural, ethical, or governance framework

Interpretations that imply these functions are non-canonical within the EntityWorks Standard.

Relationships to Other Standard Components

AI Perception is structurally related to:

  • Entity Understanding Layer (EUL) — entity-level interpretive architecture
  • AI Discoverability — downstream discipline concerned with entity resolution
  • EntityWorks Standard — the governing framework defining scope and usage
