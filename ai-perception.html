AI Perception

The broader discipline that explains how AI systems interpret and represent the world.

AI Perception is the discipline concerned with how AI systems form, update, and express their understanding of people, organisations, relationships, and ideas.

This page will become the central hub for EntityWorks’ work on AI Perception — the overarching discipline that sits above AI Discoverability. It will introduce the principles, models, and frameworks that describe how AI systems build and update their internal representations, and how that perception shapes what people see in AI-generated answers.

Full publications will be added here as the discipline is developed and formalised.
