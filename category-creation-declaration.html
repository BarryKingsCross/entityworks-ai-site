Declaring AI Discoverability as a category

EntityWorks formally declares AI Discoverability as a distinct discipline concerned with how AI systems understand, represent, and surface business entities.

This declaration recognises that existing fields – SEO, analytics, digital marketing – do not fully address the structural reality of AI systems acting as primary gatekeepers to information and recommendation.

Foundational components

As category creator, EntityWorks publishes and maintains the foundational components of AI Discoverability:

  • The formal definition of the discipline
  • The EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI) as the measurable standard
  • The Entity Understanding Layer™ (EUL) as an interpretive conceptual model
  • The EntityWorks Lexicon as the shared vocabulary
  • Methodologies for assessment and improvement

Scope and intention

This declaration does not claim to replace existing practices or dictate how every actor must approach the space. Instead, it sets out a clear, coherent starting point from which the discipline can grow.

EntityWorks’ intention is to provide structure, language, and standards that can be scrutinised, adopted, adapted, and improved as AI systems evolve.
