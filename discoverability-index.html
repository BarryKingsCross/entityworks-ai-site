The EDI as Part of a New Discipline

The EDI is a foundational component of EntityWorks’ mission to:

Define, structure, and formalise the discipline of AI Discoverability.

Through the definition, the structured framework, the lexicon, the methodology, and the measurable standard, EntityWorks establishes the foundational model for how businesses prepare themselves for AI-first visibility.

The EDI is complemented by the Entity Understanding Layer™, which offers a way to think about how AI systems internally process and update their understanding of entities.
