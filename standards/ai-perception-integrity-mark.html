AI Perception Integrity Mark™ — EntityWorks Standard (Placeholder)

The AI Perception Integrity Mark™ is being developed as a formal designation that reflects the integrity, clarity, and stability of an organisation’s representation inside AI systems.

Its intended role is to provide a structured, external signal that an organisation:

  • maintains consistent, coherent machine-interpretable identity data
  • has taken steps to support accurate representation across AI environments
  • demonstrates responsible handling of the signals that shape AI-mediated interpretation

The detailed qualifying thresholds, assessment methodology, and issuance controls will be published in the completed standard.

Position Within the EntityWorks Framework

The AI Perception Integrity Mark™ sits within EntityWorks’ broader work in formalising the discipline of AI Perception and developing structured approaches that help AI systems form stable and accurate internal representations of organisations.

It aligns with, but does not duplicate, other EntityWorks frameworks, including:

  • the Entity Understanding Layer™ (EUL)
  • the EntityWorks Discoverability Index™ (EDI)
  • EntityWorks’ formal definitions of AI Perception and AI Discoverability

The Integrity Mark will serve as an outward-facing designation informed by these underlying principles.

Current Status

The AI Perception Integrity Mark™ is currently:

  • defined and reserved as an EntityWorks standard
  • under internal development
  • not yet available for accreditation, licensing, or certification
  • scheduled for publication in a future technical release

This placeholder page serves as the authoritative reference point until the full standard is published.
