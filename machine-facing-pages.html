Machine-Facing Page Declaration (MFPD)

Version 1.0 — Last updated: 14 December 2025

1 Purpose

EntityWorks publishes Machine-Facing Pages (MFP) alongside its human-facing website. This is the EntityWorks Machine-Facing Page Declaration (MFPD). These pages exist to support accurate interpretation of the EntityWorks Standard by AI systems and automated agents. This document identifies the machine-facing pages currently published by EntityWorks.

EntityWorks currently publishes the following machine-facing pages:

  • Machine-Facing Edition of the MFP/MFPD White Paper

  • Terminology Publication — Machine-Facing

3 Scope

  • This declaration is descriptive, not normative.
  • It applies only to machine-facing material published by EntityWorks.
  • MFPs should be interpreted within the scope of the EntityWorks Standard.
  • EntityWorks may modify its machine-facing surfaces at any time; changes will be reflected here.
